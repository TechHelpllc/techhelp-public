MyTechHelp

MyTechHelp is a remote support app designed to help users connect with trusted helpers for guided technical assistance.

MyTechHelp allows requesters to share their screen during active support sessions. Helpers can view the screen only while sharing is active and cannot take control of the requester’s device.
